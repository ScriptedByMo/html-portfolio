My Best Movies

My Top 3 movies of all time

The Shawshank Redemption

This is my favourit movie. I love its powerful storytelling, strong performances , themes of hope and resilience, and its emotional impact.

Batman: The Dark Knight

Its strengths include Heath Ledger's iconic performance as the Joker, a gripping and morally complex story, and intense action sequences. The film explores deep themes like chaos vs. order, morality, and the cost of heroism, making it one of the best superhero movies ever made.

Your Name

Your Name is incredible! What I really loved about it is the way it mixes romance with fantasy and drama. The story of two teens who swap bodies across time is so unique, and it kept me hooked from start to finish.
